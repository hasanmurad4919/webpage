I worked on image and video processing in my undergraduate thesis, which led me to research on state-of-the-art deep learning techniques. I also co-authored a conference paper in which I worked on several widely used convolutional neural network models.

From 2021 to 2022, I worked as an undergraduate teaching assistant, which allowed me to assist students during lab and consultation hours. In addition, I have been honored with the distinction of receiving the Vice Chancellor's Award, being named to the Dean's List, and being awarded a scholarship based on merit.
